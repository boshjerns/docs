---
title: "All Tickers"
openapi: "GET /api/tickers"
---

Compact ticker for every asset — best bid/offer, mark, last, 24h volume, 24h change, open interest, funding rate.

Lighter than per-asset orderbook calls. Use this for dashboards showing all markets at once.

## Example

```bash
curl https://hyper.polynode.dev/api/tickers
```
